Fried Sea Cucumber Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh sea cucumbers
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Peel and clean the sea cucumbers, then slice them into 1-inch thick pieces.
  2. Season the sea cucumber slices with salt and pepper.
  3. Dip each slice into the flour, then the beaten eggs, and finally the breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Fry the sea cucumber slices until they are golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  6. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
